Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 20, 2026

This app helps you track your investments and calculate your real (inflation-adjusted) returns. We value your privacy. This policy explains what data is processed and how.

Data We Collect

The app does not collect your personal data and does not send any identifying information to our servers. In fact, we do not operate any server.

Where Your Data Is Stored

The investments, goals, and all other information you add are stored only on your device. If you choose to enable iCloud sync, this data is synced across your devices through Apple's infrastructure, within your own private iCloud account tied to your Apple ID. We cannot access this data.

Third-Party Services

The app fetches price data from Yahoo Finance to display current prices for assets such as stocks, gold, and currencies. These requests contain only asset/symbol information and none of your personal data.

Analytics and Advertising

The app contains no third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising software. Your usage is not tracked.

Children's Privacy

The app is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page.
